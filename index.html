KiwiSDR.fr

The WorldWide radio related tools website.

Section list

  • KiwiSDR.fr Map

    Our Worldwide map is not only for KiwiSDR but also for OpenWebRX, Web888, WebSDR, and PhantomSDR — and maybe soon also the AirSpy Network.

  • Analog marker/tone modulator

    Our analog modulator (or generator of tones and markers) is a flexible system capable of generating a wide range of signals. The goal is to create the most realistic and versatile modulator possible.

  • Our products

    Get here all our radio related products like plugins etc...

  • Antenna Converter/Calculator

    Calculate resonant lengths and basic parameters for common antennas: dipole, monopole, loop, folded dipole, Yagi, magnetic loop, inverted-V, 5/8λ, etc.

  • S Unit Converter

    Convert between dBm, dBW, mW, W, dBµV, dBV, dBm/Hz and estimate S-units. Includes power ↔ voltage (select resistance in Ω).

  • HackRF Driver / Gain calculator

    Quick calculator for estimating required driver/preamp gain between an SDR (e.g. HackRF) and a power amplifier (PA).

  • Our ReceiverList

    Our brand new ReceiverList is now available, all sdr from all type using the kiwisdr.fr domain or requested can choose to be displayed there or not, this is still not related to kiwisdr.com in any case!
